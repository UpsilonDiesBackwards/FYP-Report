\chapter{Project Management \& Time Keeping}

Due to extenuating circumstances, development on this artefact and paper did experience a significant delay.
I had initally spent some significant time at the beginning of the project creating the boilerplate code for the simulation,
which was partially adapted from Marmalade Engine \autocite{MarmaladeEngineMarmalade2026}, a modular Game Engine co-developed by myself.

Marmalade Engine was used as base due to my familiarity with the core structure, and to eliminate much of the time and effort it would take if I was to do it from scratch.
Furthermore, it should be noted that the code used was primarily the rendering backend, whereas nothing relating to the development of the simulation was used.

Due to weak prioritisation of my other university commitments, and the shortcomings of development early on in the supposed project timeline, the primary development window took place across the entire month of April, 2026.
